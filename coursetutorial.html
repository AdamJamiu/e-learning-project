EARTH AS A PLANETARY BODY

EARTH, OUR HOME planet, is a world unlike any other. The third planet from the sun, Earth is the only place in the known universe confirmed to host life.With a radius of 3,959 miles, Earth is the fifth largest planet in our solar system, and it's the only one known for sure to have liquid water on its surface. Earth orbits the sun once every 365.25 days . Since our calendar years have only 365 days, we add an extra leap day every four years to account for that. Earth rotates on its axis every 23.9 hours, defining day and night for surface dwellers. This axis of rotation is tilted 23.4 degrees away from the plane of Earth's orbit around the sun, giving us seasons.
